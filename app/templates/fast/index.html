Game Discription

Our game has a distinct scoring system that opens up a tactical fantasy game for fans of the UFC. Successful team managers are the ones who can build a well-balanced team so that all elements of MMA are represented. UFC fighters do not fight many times per year, but there were 46 UFC events in 2014, which generates ample matches for a fantasy game. With two separate League formats you can emphasise the UFC athletes who are near the top now or go with a “Keeper” league format, which makes you think long term. In our game team managers can also utilise Free Agents to try and steal a category they are about to lose or try and solidify areas they want to win.

  • Draft
  • Begin Season
  • End of Season and new Champion
  • Draft

Join League

Most new players on our website will of course just join a League that someone has already created. He will use Search options to find the League that he prefers. For example John Smith searches for a League that is Keeper Format, has Playoffs and minimum 10 teams. After pressing Search there will be a list of Leagues that have all his preferences and still have room for new players. John finds a League that already has seven teams so he will be the eight and only two more teams are needed to start the season.
